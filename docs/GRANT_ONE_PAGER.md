# TerraLedger — Grant & Non-Dilutive Funding One-Pager

## The Problem (1 sentence)
East African coffee exporters risk losing $11 billion in annual EU export revenue because
they cannot prove — with GPS-level precision — that their farms are deforestation-free
under the EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR, EU 2023/1115).

## Our Solution
TerraLedger is a SaaS platform that automates EUDR compliance for coffee exporters,
cooperatives, and national commodity authorities in East Africa. It fuses Sentinel-2
satellite imagery with GPS-tracked supply chain data to generate GPS-verified deforestation
scores and legally-compliant Due Diligence Statement (DDS) PDFs.

## Why Now
Large operators must comply by December 30, 2026. East Africa (Kenya, Ethiopia, Uganda,
Rwanda) represents over 41% of Africa's coffee exports to the EU. Without compliance tools,
millions of smallholder farmers face market exclusion.

## Market
- Primary: ~2,000 active coffee exporters across East Africa requiring DDS documentation
- Secondary: National commodity authorities (ECTA, UCDA, Kenya Coffee Directorate)
- Tertiary: EU importers and roasters requiring verified supplier data

## Technology
- Sentinel-2 MSI satellite data (ESA Copernicus — free, open access)
- NDVI time-series change detection against Dec 2020 deforestation baseline
- Explainable AI (XAI) layer for regulator-facing audit justification
- FastAPI backend + React dashboard + PDF DDS generation
- Ready for Google Earth Engine live integration

## Traction (Demo Stage)
- MVP built and tested: 9 real East African coffee parcels scored
- Satellite scoring engine: 10/10 automated tests passing
- DDS PDF generation: validated against EU 2023/1115 format
- Seeking design-partner cooperatives for pilot in Kenya Q3 2026
